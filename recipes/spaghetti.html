Spagetti

"Life is Better with Spaghetti"

A dish originating in Italy that combines a mixture of pasta, tomato sauce, cheese, and meatballs which satisfies a variety of cravings from sweet to savory.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 pound of dried spaghetti
  • 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
  • Freshly grated parmesan cheese (for serving)

Instructions

  1. Finely chop 1 medium yellow onion and mince 3 garlic cloves
  2. Heat 1 tablspoon olive oil in a large high-sided skillet over medium-high heat until chimmering.
  3. Add the onion, garlic, ground beef, 1 teaspoon of the kosher salt, dried oregano, black pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  4. Cook whilst breaking the beef up into small pieces with wooden spoon, until cooked through (5-7 minutes)
  5. Afterwards, carefully pour in the can crushed tomaotes and season with the remaining 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt.
  6. Bring to a boil.
  7. Then, reduce the heat to medium-low and gently simmer uncovered until the sauce is slightly thickened (~20 minutes)
  8. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  9. Add 1 pound of dried spaghetti and cook for 1 minute less than the package instructions for al dente (~9 minutes)
  10. Reserve 1 cup of the pasta water and drain the spaghetti.
  11. Return the pasta to the pot.
  12. Add the thickened sauce, pasta water, and 2 tablespoons of balsamic vinegar.
  13. Toss to combine.
  14. Bring to a simmer over medium heat whilst continuously tossing until the sauce coats the pasta and everything is evenly combined (~1-2 minutes)
  15. Serve in bowls topped with lots of grated parmesan cheese.
