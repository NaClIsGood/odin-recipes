Steak

"If it's not charred, I don't want it"

Steak : high-quality beef taken from the hindquarters of the animal, typically cut into thick slices that are cooked by broiling or frying.

Ingredients

  • 1 beef flat iron steak or boneless top sirloin steak (3/4 pound)
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened, divided
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix 1 tablespoon butter with paresley, garlic and soy sauce
  2. Sprinkle steak with salt and pepper
  3. In a large skillet, heat remaining butter over medium heat
  4. Add steak, cook until meat reaches desired doneness (medium rare = 135 degrees)
  5. Let steak rest for 5 minutes
  6. Serve with garlic butter
